Avatar: The Way of Water

⭐⭐⭐⭐(6.9/10.0)

The Way of Water follows the journey of Jake Sully and Neytiri's newfound family of children . Despite their best efforts to keep their family safe, a familiar threat resurfaces because Earth is dying, and forces the family to become refugees and flee to the land of the Metkayina clan in the Pandoran oceans.
